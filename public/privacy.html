6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share data with:

  • Logging service: Central logging for operational logs
  • Database: Shared PostgreSQL for session and result storage

We do not sell your personal data. Third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements where required by GDPR.

7. International Transfers

If data is transferred outside the EEA, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or adequacy decisions.

8. Data Retention

  • Anonymous session data: Kept only as long as needed to provide the shopping session and short operational troubleshooting.
  • Account session data: Available to the signed-in user until the user deletes it or requests anonymization through the account privacy controls.
  • Anonymization: Account-linked session identifiers can be detached from the account, and stored query/message/search-run text is replaced with anonymized markers while non-personal merchant result records may remain for service integrity.
  • Deletion: Signed-in users can delete their Shop Assistant session, profile, saved-search, message, result, and choice data from the account privacy endpoint.
  • Logs: Operational logs follow the central logging retention policy and should contain metadata only, not raw search text, JWTs, or secrets.

A repository-owned retention runner is available for anonymous session cleanup: npm run retention:cleanup defaults to dry-run, targets only anonymous sessions, uses an environment-configurable TTL and batch size, and requires an explicit apply confirmation before deletion. Production scheduling and deployment of that runner still require owner-approved operations wiring.
