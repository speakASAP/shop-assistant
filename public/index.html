From request to decision.

The service turns messy shopping intent into comparable offers, then keeps the full trail available to the right customer or operator.

1

Describe

Customers type or dictate what they need, including size, region, budget, delivery constraints, and quality preferences.

2

Compare

AI extracts intent, searches merchant pages, ranks offers, and keeps budget, delivery, quality, brand, or size refinements one click away.

3

Decide

Each merchant redirect can save the selected product, so customers can reopen the seller or refine alternatives from the dashboard.

Every customer gets a private workspace.

The dashboard is built around authenticated history: searches, selected offers, follow-up refinements, reusable saved criteria, and profiles for different buyers.

✓Authenticated `/api/me` endpoints keep dashboard data scoped to the signed-in customer.
✓Saved searches and profiles can be reused for family, teams, or recurring purchasing patterns.
✓Expired or invalid tokens are cleared instead of showing private dashboard data.
