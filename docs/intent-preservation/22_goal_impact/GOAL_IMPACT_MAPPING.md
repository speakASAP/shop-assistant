## Goals

- SA-G1: Request-to-result quality.
- SA-G2: Legal and privacy compliance.
- SA-G3: Multi-intent and profile search.
- SA-G4: Agent admin and observability.
- SA-G5: Lead capture integrations.
- SA-G6: Operations and deployment.

## Mapping Rule

Every task must map to at least one goal and declare expected impact, invariant impact, sensitive-data classification, contract impact, replay or determinism impact, and validation evidence.
