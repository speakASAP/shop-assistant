## Sequencing Strategy

1. Preserve and audit original intent documentation.
2. Improve search quality from failed-search evidence.
3. Produce UX improvement evidence from session data.
4. Strengthen profiles, saved criteria, and multi-product workflows based on validated usage.
5. Maintain legal, privacy, and operational readiness gates before deployment.
